Who We Are

A Family Business Built on Trust

Founded in 2010, JK Window Decorations started with a simple belief — that every home deserves beautiful, well-crafted window treatments. What began as word-of-mouth referrals quickly grew into one of Thiruvananthapuram's most recognised names in interior window solutions.

We work with homeowners, interior designers, architects, corporate offices, and healthcare facilities. From the initial consultation to the final installation, our team is involved at every step to ensure a seamless, high-quality outcome.

Our products are sourced from trusted suppliers, combining durability with aesthetic appeal. We take pride in our after-sales service — because a good relationship doesn't end at installation.
